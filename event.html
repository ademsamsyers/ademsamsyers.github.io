PAST HIGHLIGHT

KL BIKE WEEK 2025

HERITAGE VALLEY KUALA LUMPUR: 19 - 20 DECEMBER 2025  

KL Bike Week 2025 is an annual motorcycle festival held on 19–20 December 2025 at Heritage Valley, Kuala Lumpur, bringing together local and international riders through custom bike showcases, motorcycle club gatherings, lifestyle vendors, live music, and a strong sense of brotherhood under the theme “Familia Love.”

 

ASIA HARLEY DAYS 2025 

MIDWINTER: 21 - 22 NOVEMBER 2025 

Asia Harley Days 2025 was held at Midwinter, Khao Yai, in Nakhon Ratchasima, Thailand on 21–22 November 2025, bringing together thousands of Harley-Davidson riders, H.O.G. chapters and motorcycle enthusiasts from across Asia for a two-day motorcycle and music festival with parades, stunt shows, live performances and lifestyle exhibits.
