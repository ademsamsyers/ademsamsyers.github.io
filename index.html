HARLEY DAVIDSON

Our Heritage

Harley Davidson is one of the most iconic motorcycle manufacturers in the world, founded in 1903 in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, USA. With over a century of history, the brand has become a global symbol of freedom, power, and individuality.

Today, Harley-Davidson continues to evolve by combining its classic heritage with modern technology, performance innovation, and new models designed for the next generation of riders.

The highlight

  • Founded in 1903, one of the oldest motorcycle brands in the world
  • Known for V-Twin engines and iconic design
  • Strong global rider community and lifestyle culture
  • Wide range of motorcycles: cruiser, touring, sport, and adventure
  • A symbol of freedom, independence, and brotherhood
