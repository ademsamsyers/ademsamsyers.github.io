PRIVACY POLICY

1. INTRODUCTION

Harley-Davidson® Petaling Jaya (Telagamas Mobility Sdn Bhd) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA) of Malaysia.

2. DATA WE COLLECT

We may collect personal information that is necessary for our business functions, including but not limited to:

  • Full name, contact number, and home address.
  • Identity card (IC) or passport details for vehicle registration and insurance.
  • Financial information and documents for loan applications.
  • Motorcycle preferences, service history, and riding data.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

Your information is processed for the following purposes:

  • Facilitating motorcycle sales, trade-ins, and financing.
  • Managing service appointments and technical support.
  • Sending updates on official H-D events, promotions, and new arrivals.
  • Compliance with legal obligations and safety recall notifications.

4. DATA DISCLOSURE

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share your information with Harley-Davidson Motor Company, insurance providers, and financial institutions to complete your transactions.

5. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to access, correct, or limit the processing of your personal data. Any requests can be directed to our data management team.
